## Sequencing recommendation (first sprint after 30-day review)

**Sprint 1 (P1 heavy — 10 days):** CB-01 triage · RP-01 GST returns · CP-01 quote e-signature · VP-01 PO ack · FE-03 reminder ladder.
**Sprint 2 (perf + AI — 10 days):** PI-01 pg_trgm · PI-02 activity partitioning · AI-01 streaming · AI-06 budget guardrails.
**Sprint 3 (mobile + inventory — 10 days):** MO-01 responsive audit · IN-01 multi-warehouse groundwork · MF-04 QC photos · IS-02 snag list.

Beyond sprint 3, sequencing depends on production usage data (which dashboards are hit, which reports are exported, which vendors log in, which mobile screens are used).
